About Us

Wasan Group was established in 1961 and is among India’s most trusted conglomerates in the automobile sector. Across the past six decades, Wasan Group has emerged as a highly diversified group of companies. We are associated with a few of the world’s leading automobile brands such as Daimler, Toyota, Ford, Tata, Bajaj, and Mahindra.

Wasan Group prides itself in the high values that it stands for, and the group's 1000 plus workforce ensures that they uphold these values in every possible way.

Who We Are

Wasan Group is proud to present Everi, a company that changes the way India rides pre-owned two-wheelers. As an initiative to provide top-quality bikes of every make and model for everybody at every price, Everi delivers trust and credibility to the used vehicles market.

An innovative store with a dual-platform presence, Everi steps into the untapped second-hand two-wheeler market with our industry experience. Why Choose Everi ?

The Indian used two-wheeler market has experienced strong growth over the past decade. But, it is largely dominated by unorganized players which consist of sellers, local mechanics, brokers, and small dealerships.

We understand that trusting a second-hand vehicle can be difficult for you. But with our 6- month warranty against any technical malfunction, you can be rest assured that your family is safe.

A dedicated crew of engineers works diligently to present to you proper, refurbished motorcycles so that you get the look and feel of a new vehicle at really cheap prices. Our squad aims at giving you your dream bike at hassle-free terms and the best prices. We have only one goal: "Every Make, Every Model, For Everybody, Every Budget"
